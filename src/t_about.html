about fb.mu/

fb.mu/ allows you to shorten links (URLs) making them accessible for your Facebook friends only. Feel free to post the short link on twitter or any public blog - only your friends will be able to use it.

For example, you may wish to shorten a link like "www.best-restaurant-in-town.com/address.html", and then post on twitter: "folks, my birthday party is here http://fb.mu/3jKe". Your friends will be directed to the restaurant. Not friends will be denied. Try this out yourself.

Businesses may enjoy sending links to discounts and promotions, and encourage potential customers to make friends with business employees or owners.

the story behind

fb.mu/ is actually a one-man over-weekend effort. Current team consists of the only person, @arbkv. My motivation was actually to quick start with some technologies I plan to use on another project.

The site uses two cloud services as a platform:

- Google App Engine (http://code.google.com/appengine/), and
- Facebook Open Graph API (http://developers.facebook.com/)

Entire site code is about 170 lines of python code, and two html files. I started one Friday evening, and uploaded the production version same Sunday night. Entire project took about 30 hours of work, including: domain name purchase and setup, App Engine python and Django development, learning Open Graph API, and implementing Facebook authorization, website graphics design, html, css, and javascript coding, testing, and deployment. My only equipment were paper, pencil, coffee, MacBook Pro and a text editor.
